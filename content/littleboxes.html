Numbers as Boxes

Let's think of numbers as groups of little boxes. For example, here is the number 23

There are 23 little boxes. Count them to be sure! They are arranged as:

  • 2 groups of 10 and
  • 3 groups of 1
You could also write it as an equation: \( 2 \times 10 + 3 \times 1 = 23 \)
