Devotion Math League Bases: Exponents

Exponents are VERY IMPORTANT for Bases Problems.

Example: \( 4^3 \) means \( 4 \times 4 \times 4 = 64\)

  1. \( 3^2 \) means __________________
  2. \( 2^3 \) means __________________
  3. \( 2^4 \) means __________________

What does: \( 4^0 \) mean?

Anything "to the zero" equals 1. So \( 4^0 = 1 \).
