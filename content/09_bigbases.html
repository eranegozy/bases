Bases 9: Bases Larger Than 10

Big Bases

Bases can go bigger than 10! We will look at bases that go up to Base 16.
We need symbols for a single digit that is bigger than 10. We use the letters A, B, C, D, E, and F.


symbol value
A 10
B 11
C 12
D 13
E 14
F 15
